# Code of conduct

MyCogni contributors must make participation safe, respectful, and privacy-preserving.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence without personal attacks or harassment.
- Respect different experience levels, identities, threat models, and accessibility needs.
- Accept technical challenge and revise claims when evidence changes.
- Keep private information private. Never pressure somebody to disclose their identity, location, threat situation, broker record, or legal documents.
- Use synthetic examples and reserved domains in all public collaboration.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing, threats, impersonation, deliberate disclosure of personal data, malicious connector submissions, or attempts to use the project against an unauthorized person are prohibited.

## Enforcement

Report conduct issues privately to the repository owner. Do not include another person's PII in the report unless strictly necessary for immediate safety. Maintainers may edit or remove content, reject changes, restrict participation, revoke connector trust, or ban an account. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate and may remain private to protect affected people.

This policy applies in project repositories, issue trackers, review channels, community forums, and public representation of the project.
